1995-2011

Biography

Steve Jobs was an American business magnate, industrial designer, and investor. He was the co-founder, chairman, and CEO of Apple Inc., a pioneer in the personal computer revolution of the 1970s and 1980s. His innovative contributions to technology, design, and business changed the way people use technology in their daily lives.Jobs was known for his attention to detail and focus on user experience, leading to the creation of some of the most iconic products in tech history, including the iPhone, iPod, iPad, and the Macintosh computer. His relentless pursuit of perfection and his unique vision made him one of the most influential figures of the modern world.
